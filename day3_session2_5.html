Slide 2 • Core System

The CRAFT Framework

Click on each letter card below to analyze the recipe
components of a masterful prompt.
C

Context (Background Information)

Context sets the stage for the AI. It outlines the *why*, provides background constraints, and clarifies parameters so the AI doesn't have to guess the scenario.

Example Element: "...while relating the science concepts specifically to daily household routines."

R is for Role

By assigning a Role, you cue the AI to adopt a specific expert perspective, tuning its vocabulary, vocabulary level, and analytical framework.

Slide 5 • Adaptability Layer

A is for Audience

To construct optimal prompt results, we must teach the AI exactly who is consuming the information. Great teachers naturally adapt explanations to their audience. AI does the same!

Rule of Thumb: Always tell AI who your final readers are.

Topic: Artificial Intelligence

Simulated Explanation:
"AI is like a very smart puppy that learns tricks from looking at thousands of examples instead of you teaching it one by one!"
