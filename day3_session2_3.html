Slide 1: Introduction

What is Generative AI?

Instead of just matching or finding existing data (like traditional software), Generative AI can invent, synthesize, and construct completely brand new content out of learned patterns.

Traditional Software: Follows rigid, pre-programmed paths with mathematical precision (same input always gives same output).
Generative AI: Creates brand new, dynamic assets dynamically (requires intentional guidance & rules).

Slide 3: Essential Needs

Why AI Needs Prompts

Imagine opening a chat box and entering: "Write something." What does the AI do? It has to blindly guess your thoughts!

Without structured guidance, AI has no baseline context. Prompts act as a navigational map providing four key directions:

🎯 Goal Definition 🗺️ Clear Context 👥 Specific Audience 📐 Formatting Structure
