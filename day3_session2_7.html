Slide 51 • Interactive Exercise

Prompt Evolution: Teach Me Chemistry

How do we optimize a vague, simple prompt into a highly tailored professional command?

Standard Level

Vague & Simple

"Teach me chemistry."

Result: The AI drops an overly academic textbook dump or basic description.

Developing Level

Role & Context Added

"You are an organic chemistry professor. Teach me high school-level chemical reactions."

Result: Better tone adjustment and accurate difficulty level sizing.

Elite Prompt Level

Perfect Formula Block

"You are a master chemistry tutor. Explain ionic bonding using baking ingredients as an analogy, then give me 3 practice multiple choice questions."

Result: Maximum output customization, memorable analogies, and immediate quizzes.

Healthcare Discussion: Ethics & Safety

Let's debate: "What tasks in healthcare should always involve humans, and what is the limit of AI?"

High-Risk Tasks (Human Only)

  • Primary treatment/diagnostic decisions
  • Delivering emotionally sensitive diagnoses
  • Custom surgeries & critical clinical calls

Suitable AI Assisted Tasks

  • Drafting discharge letters and administrative summaries
  • Cross-checking pharmacological drug interactions
  • Translating complex medical papers into simplified formats
