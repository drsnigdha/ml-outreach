5 Rules for AI Responsibility

  • Verify critical claims using authoritative, verified primary directories.
  • Never input sensitive personal information, credentials, or private keys.
  • Disclose AI involvement in creative assignments; don't plagiarize.
  • Reference creators and give credit when building from source concepts.
  • Leverage generative technology to enhance your learning scope, not skip it.
